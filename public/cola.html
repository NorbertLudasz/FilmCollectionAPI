About cola

Coca-Cola, or Coke, is a carbonated soft drink manufactured by The Coca-Cola Company. Originally marketed as a temperance drink and intended as a patent medicine, it was invented in the late 19th century by John Stith Pemberton in Atlanta, Georgia.

About sales

Revenue rose nine per cent year-over-year in 2019 to $37.3bn, driven by concentrate sales growth of one per cent and price/mix growth of five per cent. Organic revenue grew six per cent year-over-year, operating margin improved from 26.7 to 27.1 per cent, and earnings per share rose 38 per cent to $2.07.
